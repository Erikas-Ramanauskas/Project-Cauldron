Rules

  • At the outset the player is assigned vitality points as well as levels for strength, agility and dexterity.

  • In each round the player will face an enemy with its own strength, agility and dexterity levels.

  • To defeat an enemy the player must have higher levels than the enemy in each category??????

  • If you defeat an enemy you proceed to the next round. If you are defeated you lose vitality points before proceeding to the next round. The game is over when you lose all your vitality points.

  • At the outset you are also assigned ingredients. As you move through the rounds you will also be rewarded with further ingredients. These ingredients can be used to make potions. The potions can then be used to increase your levels or decrease those of your enemy as per the properties of the particular potion.

  • At any point a player can enter the Witches Kitchen with the ingredients they currently have and experiment by mixing them in the cauldron to find potions. Possible potions and properties will be recorded in the recipe book.

  • Prior to commencing any fight with an enemy the player can mix and use a potion based on the ingredients they currently have. Choose wisely. The ingredients are used up once the potion is used.

  • Use your ingredients wisely. Win as many fights as you can. Stay alive!
